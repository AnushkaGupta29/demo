My Trip to Indore

Indore is the largest city in Madhya Pradesh, a state in central India, and it is the cleanest city in the state. There are many places to visit, such as Ralamandal, Patalpani, Lotus Valley, Rajwada Palace, and Kshatri. Indore is also famous for its food, particularly the famous "Indori Poha." There is a food place known as "Chhapan Dukan," which is famous for its 56 different food items.

Ujjain, which is nearby, is famous for the Mahakaleshwar Temple. Another nearby place is Omkareshwar.
